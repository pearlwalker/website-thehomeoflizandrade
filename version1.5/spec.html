FAMILY OF 3 FOUND IN BUNKER

What was thought to be an abandoned nuclear shelter turns out to be the family home of the ANDRADE family.

Mr. & Mrs. Andrade (names and ages unknown) moved to the property, where they then had a litter of two (Rui Andrade, male, 8; Marielle Andrade, female, 8 years old). The couple raised their litter through young adulthood while on the property, when the daughter seems to have moved out. Rui remained on the property after the death of the original homeowners as the bunker’s sole occupant.

Before moving back onto the property, Marielle Andrade allegedly attempted to bring criminal charges against her ex-partner before the case was dropped. The alleged charges include: Domestic and Economic Abuse, Assault and Battery, Improper Disclosure of Medical Records, Medical Fraud Conspiracy, and Wrongful Death of a Newborn. Prosecution was not able to prove intent or liability on the part of the defendant, leading to the court dropping the case.

Marielle Andrade moved back to the bunker this summer. Shortly after, she adopted an abandoned kitten (Liz Andrade, female, less than 1yrs), bringing the property’s total occupants to 3 cats. Local pediatrician Marcos Oliviera (male, 6yrs), the cat who informed local government of the property’s status, seemed concerned about the health and wellbeing of the child.
